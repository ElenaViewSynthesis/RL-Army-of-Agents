### 3. Recommended Retention Structure

| Parameter | Recommendation | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| **Retention (Deductible)** | **£1.0M (1.25% of EV / 8.3% of £12M limit)** | Market norm for complex mid-market: 0.75–1.5% EV. £1M ensures buyer retains meaningful first-loss exposure for attritional warranty breaches (working capital, accounts accuracy, minor commercial contracts). |
| **Structure** | **Tipping Basket** | Once aggregate validated claims exceed £1M, insurer pays from first pound. Standard European market practice; aligns buyer/insurer incentives on claims validation. |
| **De Minimis** | **£100,000 per individual claim** | Filters nuisance claims; reduces administrative burden. Aggregates toward basket. |
| **Fundamental / Tax Warranty Retention** | **Same basket (tipping applies across all warranties)** | Alternative: separate £250k fundamental/tax basket — adds complexity; single basket preferred for admin simplicity unless buyer insists. |

**Seller Retention (if sell-side policy considered):** Not applicable — buy-side is market standard. Sell-side would require seller to fund retention from escrow; rarely used for fintech regulatory risk.

### 4. Key Exclusions — Regulatory & AI-Specific

*The following exclusions are **minimum required** for this risk profile. Additional exclusions will emerge from diligence review.*

#### A. GDPR / Data Protection

| Exclusion | Scope | Negotiability |
|---|---|---|
| **GDPR Administrative Fines (Art. 83)** | Direct fines imposed by ICO / EU SAAs on the target/controller | **Hard exclusion** — uninsurable as a matter of public policy in UK/EU (penal vs compensatory). Insurer cannot indemnify regulatory penalties. |
| **GDPR Compensatory Damages (Art. 82)** | Private claims by data subjects for material/non-material damage | **Coverable in principle** — but sub-limited to £2M regulatory sub-limit; subject to: (a) warranty breach causal link, (b) known claims excluded, (c) ongoing compliance warranty forward-looking carve-out. |
| **Pre-Completion Data Subject Access Request (DSAR) Backlog** | Known or reasonably discoverable backlog at completion | **Hard exclusion** if disclosed in DD; if undisclosed, covered subject to warranty breach proof. |
| **International Data Transfer Mechanism Invalidity** | Schrems II / UK adequacy / SCC failure risk | **Excluded** — macro-legal risk outside target's control. Buyer assumes transfer mechanism risk post-completion. |
| **AI Training Data Provenance** | Copyright / licence / consent defects in training datasets | **Excluded** pending specialist IP/tech diligence. If clean FTO opinion provided, may be covered with sub-limit. |

#### B. Financial Services Regulation (FCA / PRA)

| Exclusion | Scope | Negotiability |
|---|---|---|
| **FCA/PRA Enforcement Fines & Penalties** | Direct regulatory sanctions | **Hard exclusion** — public policy (penal). |
| **Regulatory Capital / Liquidity Shortfalls** | Breach of capital requirements, wind-down plan deficiencies | **Excluded** — forward-looking business risk, not warranty breach. |
| **Permission Variation / Revocation Risk** | FCA varying or cancelling permissions post-completion | **Excluded** — supervisory discretion; not a warranty breach unless specific "no undisclosed regulatory proceedings" warranty breached. |
| **Consumer Duty / Senior Managers Regime Non-Compliance** | Post-completion implementation gaps | **Excluded** to extent forward-looking; covered only if *pre-completion* breach of "compliance with all laws" warranty proven. |
| **Known Supervisory Findings / Skilled Person Reports (S166)** | Any matter disclosed in DD or regulatory correspondence | **Hard exclusion** — known risk. |

#### C. AI / Technology Specific

| Exclusion | Scope | Negotiability |
|---|---|---|
| **Model Performance / Accuracy Drift** | AI model degradation, bias emergence, concept drift post-completion | **Excluded** — forward-looking performance warranty not insurable. |
| **Third-Party Model Dependencies** | Open-source / API / foundation model licence breaches (e.g., OpenAI, HuggingFace) | **Excluded** unless specific IP warranty breached and diligence confirms clean chain. |
| **Algorithmic Discrimination / Bias Claims** | Equality Act 2010 / EU AI Act (Art. 5 prohibited practices) claims | **Sub-limited to regulatory sub-limit**; excluded if arising from post-completion deployment changes. |
| **Cyber / Ransomware / Data Breach Losses** | First-party (business interruption, restoration) and third-party (regulatory, notification) | **Excluded** — separate cyber policy required. W&I covers *warranty breach* re: cyber security representations only, not the breach event itself. |
| **Key Person / IP Departure** | Founder/CTO exit post-completion | **Excluded** — commercial risk. Coverable only via specific "key employee retention" warranty breach. |

#### D. Standard Market Exclusions (Reiterated for Clarity)

- Fraud / wilful misconduct by insured or target management (seller fraud cover available at +15–25bp ROL)
- Forward-looking warranties (forecasts, projections, "will continue to comply")
- Purchase price adjustments / completion accounts / earn-out disputes
- Anti-trust / merger control clearance failure
- Nuclear, war, terrorism, sanctions (standard Lloyd's MAJ clause)
- Pollution / environmental (unless specific environmental liability policy)
- Pension scheme deficits (Section 75 debts, PPF levy) — separate pension trustee policy

### 8. Senior Underwriter Commentary

> **This risk sits at the intersection of three hardening market themes: fintech regulatory scrutiny, AI governance uncertainty, and GDPR enforcement escalation.** The 3.00–3.50% blended ROL reflects genuine loss cost uncertainty — not market cycle opportunism. The £1M tipping basket is the minimum to align incentives; we would not entertain nil retention. The regulatory fine exclusion is non-negotiable (public policy). Compensatory GDPR damages are coverable but must be sub-limited and causally linked to a *pre-completion* warranty breach — not a post-completion compliance failure.
>
> **Critical diligence gap to close:** FCA authorisation status. If the target operates under temporary permissions or is unregistered, the regulatory warranty framework collapses and we would likely decline or restrict to fundamental/title only. Early clarity on this is essential.
>
> **Recommendation to buyer:** Budget £400k–£500k total W&I cost (premium + adviser fees). Negotiate seller escrow ≥ £3M. Commission a pre-sign AI governance audit — it reduces our exclusion list and your post-deal integration risk.
